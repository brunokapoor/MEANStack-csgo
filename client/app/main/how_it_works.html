Flow of sale

  1. Select an item from your invetory and click "Place item"
  2. Accept the trade with the CSGOSM bot which will keep the skin until somebody buys it
  3. Your skin will be put on sale
  4. When an user buys the skin you will receive the earnings

Flow of Purchase

  1. Select one or more items to purchase
  2. Go to the cart and click buy
  3. After the payment is completed you will receive a trading request from our CSGOSM bot
  4. Once the trade is accepted the new skin will be available in your inventory. You can check your inventory in the User Dashboard

Before buying

Before being able to buy a skin you will need to:

  1. Set up your trade url in the settings page
  2. Add funds to your account

Adding funds to your account

To be able to purchase skins you will firstly need to add funds to your account. To do this click on "Make a deposit" under the user menu on the top right corner once logged in.

You can select different ammounts to deposit. All transactions are being dealt by Paypal at the current time.

Withdrawing funds

You can cashout funds to Paypal by going to the withdrawals page under the user menu on the top right corner once logged in and adding a new withdrawal.

Please note that all withdrawals are manually reviewed for security reasons and it can take up to 7 days to receive your funds.

There is a minimun withdrawal ammount which is $5.00 USD.

Problems and support

If you are experiecing problems like items missing from your inventory or any other problems please make a post in our support forums or contact the live chat.
